About


I am an adaptable professional with excellent track record. I successfully executed several key high value contracts for energy majors in over 25 countries. I have mixed field and office experience spanning over nine years in the international upstream oil and gas services industry engaging diverse teams on high risk offshore projects. I went to university in Tecnologico de Monterrey where I majored in Electronic Systems Engineering. Currently I am finalizing a master’s degree in Engineering specialized in quality and productivity with Six Sigma Black Belt certification.

After years wandering around the globe for work now I aim to stay in one place and continue traveling just for pleasure. I have visited 55 of the Mexican magic towns, still 56 to go… When ever I get an opportunity I escape to the sea. I love Scuba diving, you can lose yourself to the beauty of the sea and escape reality. I love the calm, the colors and the life.
